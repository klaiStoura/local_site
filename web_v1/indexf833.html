ABOUT POPMYDAY

Founded by two HEC Graduates, Morgane L'Hostis and Charles Berenguer, Popmyday is a new mobile application that allows you to book in-home beauty and wellness services, anywhere, anytime, at the tap of a button.

Either at home or at your office, after work hours or during the weekend. Popmyday services range from makeup to hair and nails. All the professionals (called "Popartist") are screened, tested and hand-picked to guarantee a high standard service.

The service opened in Paris area in Fall 2014. Bookings are available through an iPhone and Android application or a responsive website www.popmyday.com
